Header: Organization ID,VIN,State,Transaction Date,Transaction Month

Column Definitions
    Organization ID: A provided org identifier. Used to load data into private org HDS.
    VIN: Vehicle Identification Number.
    State: State Abbreviation 2 capital letters.
    Transaction Date: Date the data was extracted from org.
    Transaction Month: Format YYYY-MM, MM required. The month that is asserted to have coverage.


Coverage: A vehicle is expected to be insured for at least 15 days of the coming month.
